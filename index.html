I am a self-taught video editor known for introducing innovative editing styles that create new trends in content creation. I think I kicked off my journey back in 2018, since then I was super passionate to turn regular videos into cool, engaging stories. I've had an amazing stroke of luck; over almost a decade, and great feedbacks from my clients has just continued to drive my love for the work. I use Adobe After Effects for most of the edits I do as I believe it is the leading software for creating fantastic visual effects, lively 2D and text animation, complex compositing, tasteful motion graphics, and pinpoint video stabilization. I do multi-camera editing, and with Adobe Premiere Pro, there's also the playing around with audio editing and colour correction. This is to ensure that small parts of the video are accorded serious attention and precision.

Advanced compositing, motion tracking, color grading, rotoscoping, 2D animation integration, visual effects, and sophisticated transitions are among my areas of expertise. Having these skills allows me to bring to life any idea no matter how complex it might be.

What really matters in this line of work to me is creativity. I really try to look for new styles of edit which would catch anybody's interest. My goal is to create videos that leave viewers in awe and linger in their thoughts. My process heavily relies on keeping open communication and actually understanding what my clients are trying to achieve. Teamwork that ensures the final product really captures the client's vibe and message. I am all about learning new skills, staying current on what's hot in video editing, so I can provide my clients with a stellar experience.
